=== Mention Weight Empirical Calibration ===

Methodology:
  For each retweet (A->B), compute corr(A_sentiment, B_recent_7d_mean)
  For each mention (A->B), compute corr(A_sentiment, B_recent_7d_mean)
  Mention weight = mention_corr / retweet_corr, clipped to [0.1, 1.0]

Results:
  Retweet pairs analysed: 1780
  Retweet sentiment correlation: r = 0.1200
  Mention pairs analysed: 4897
  Mention sentiment correlation: r = 0.1000
  Raw ratio: 0.8329
  Calibrated mention weight: w_m = 0.833

Interpretation:
  Mentions carry nearly as much sentiment alignment as retweets.
  The two edge types are relatively interchangeable.
